Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

What services does Illumination Data Solutions offer?
Illumination Data Solutions offers comprehensive data science services including traditional analytics (Data Analysis, Machine Learning, Business Intelligence, Data Visualization, Data Engineering) and cutting-edge generative AI solutions (Custom Chatbots, AI Agent Design, Agentic Systems, AI Image Generation, LLM Integration & RAG, Computer Vision, Cloud Solutions & MLOps, and Training & Consulting).
How can I request a quote for my data science project?
You can request a quote by visiting our "Contact" page or emailing us directly at info@illuminationdata.com. We offer free initial consultations to understand your needs and provide customized project estimates.
Does Illumination Data Solutions handle both small and large-scale projects?
Yes, Illumination Data Solutions handles both small and large-scale projects. Whether you need assistance with a simple data analysis task, a custom chatbot, or a large-scale enterprise AI system with multiple agents, we have the expertise, resources, and capabilities to meet your needs. Our experienced team can tailor services to accommodate projects of various sizes and complexities, ensuring quality results regardless of the scale.
What sets Illumination Data Solutions apart from other data science consulting firms?
Cutting-Edge Expertise: We specialize in both traditional data science and next-generation AI including custom chatbots, AI agents, and generative systems.

Ethical AI Focus: We prioritize responsible AI development with transparency, fairness, and client empowerment at the core of our approach.

Personalized Solutions: As a boutique firm, we provide tailored solutions rather than one-size-fits-all approaches, ensuring each project meets specific business needs.
Can you integrate AI agents and chatbots with existing business systems?
Absolutely! We specialize in seamless integration of AI agents, chatbots, and agentic systems with existing business infrastructure. Our solutions work with popular platforms, APIs, databases, and can be deployed on cloud services like AWS. We ensure minimal disruption to your current workflows while maximizing the benefits of AI automation.
How long does a typical data science project take to complete?
Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple data analysis or basic chatbot development may take 1-4 weeks. Medium complexity projects like custom AI agents, advanced visualizations, or machine learning models typically require 1-3 months. Large-scale implementations such as enterprise agentic systems, comprehensive data platforms, or complex AI image generation solutions can take 3-6 months or more.
Do you provide training and support after project completion?
Yes! We provide comprehensive training programs to help your team effectively use and maintain AI solutions. Our support includes documentation, user training sessions, ongoing technical support, and knowledge transfer to ensure your organization can independently manage the implemented systems. We also offer retainer-based support for continued assistance.
What types of businesses benefit most from your generative AI services?
Our generative AI services benefit diverse industries including e-commerce (chatbots, product recommendations), healthcare (data analysis, automated reporting), education (personalized learning systems), marketing (image generation, content creation), and finance (fraud detection, automated decision systems). Any business looking to automate processes, enhance customer engagement, or leverage AI for competitive advantage can benefit from our solutions.
What does Illumination Data Solutions projects cost?
Project costs vary based on complexity, timeline, and specific requirements. Basic analytics projects may start around $5,000-$15,000, while custom chatbots and AI agents typically range from $15,000-$50,000. Complex agentic systems, enterprise ML solutions, and comprehensive data platforms can range from $50,000-$200,000+. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs and offer flexible payment structures.
